**Effective Date:** February 12, 2026

HarvestHomeGuides.com ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. This Cookie Policy explains the types of cookies we use, their purposes, and how you can manage your preferences.

#### Types of Cookies

- **Necessary Cookies:** Essential for the operation of the site. Without these cookies, services you have asked for cannot be provided.

- **Performance Cookies:** Help us understand how visitors interact with our site by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This data helps us improve the performance of our website.

- **Functional Cookies:** Allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your region or preferences) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

#### Purposes of Cookies

- **Necessary Cookies:** Ensure the site functions correctly.
- **Performance Cookies:** Improve site performance and user experience.
- **Functional Cookies:** Enhance user experience by remembering preferences.

#### Cookie Management

You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you choose to disable cookies, some features of our site may not function properly.

#### Third-Party Cookies

- **Google Analytics (GA4):** We use Google Analytics 4 for tracking and reporting website traffic. GA4 uses first-party cookies (such as `_ga` and `_ga_*`) to distinguish unique users and throttle request rates. These cookies collect anonymized data about how you use our site. For more information on how Google collects and processes data, please visit the [Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

- **SendFox:** We use SendFox for our email newsletter signup forms. When you interact with our signup forms, SendFox may set cookies to process your subscription and prevent spam. For more information, visit [SendFox's Privacy Policy](https://sendfox.com/privacy).

#### Data Protection

Data collected through cookies is stored securely and used in accordance with our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/).

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
