# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 12, 2026*

## Who We Are

Harvest Home Guides is a publication of EPM Labs, LLC. Our website is [www.harvesthomeguides.com](https://www.harvesthomeguides.com).

## Information We Collect

### Analytics
We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects:
- Pages visited and time spent
- General geographic location (country/region level)
- Device type and browser
- Referral source

Google Analytics uses cookies. You can opt out using [Google's opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Email Newsletter
If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. We use this solely to send you gardening tips, book updates, and occasional promotions. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.

### Purchases
We sell books through Amazon (KDP). We do not process payments directly. Amazon's privacy policy governs purchases made through their platform.

## How We Use Information

- To improve our website content and user experience
- To send newsletters to subscribers who opt in
- To understand which content is most helpful to our readers

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- Google Analytics (anonymized usage data)
- Our email service provider (to deliver newsletters)

## Cookies

Our site uses cookies for:
- Google Analytics (traffic analysis)
- Essential site functionality

## Your Rights

You may:
- Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
- Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
- Request deletion of your data by emailing us

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at [hello@harvesthomeguides.com](mailto:hello@harvesthomeguides.com).
