**Skipping mulch.** Bare soil in spring means weeds, moisture loss, and rapidly climbing soil temperatures. Mulch 3â€“4 inches deep after planting. Pull it back slightly from stems to prevent rot.

**Overwatering.** New gardeners water every day. Established vegetable gardens in spring typically need 1 inch per week. Water deeply, less often. Check soil moisture at 2 inches deep before watering â€” if it's moist, wait.
